How does it work?


Kotoa smart home consists of a smart hub, smart sensors, and devices controlled by smart power adapters. Smart nodes such as sensors and power adapters have 6LoWPAN connectivity to each other. The smart hub has Wi-Fi connectivity to the smartphone application and the cloud service, and also 6LoWPAN connectivity to the smart nodes. Kotoa can understand your preference, do routine housework and carry out instant command rapidly, automatically and accurately.
