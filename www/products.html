Hub


Kotoa smart home can connect your home to the Internet with Hub, so that you can reach your Kotoa at cloud or by mobile equipments, especially when you are not at home.


Power Adapter


Power adapter is the device which controls the states of one electrical appliance or system directly such as on or off.




Sensor


Sensors are devices that measure physical quantities such as temperature and humidity, and convert them into signals which can be read by Kotoa.


Motion Detector


Motion Detector is a kind of sensor module that detects moving objects, particularly people and send alerts to Kotoa so that emergency can be handled.




Microphone


You can use microphone to control the equipments in your home by voice. Kotoa can record and understand your voice, then take relative action.


Speaker


When Kotoa are equipped with some speaker, she can communicate with you by voice.
